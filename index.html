About Me

Chris Kim, Retail Group Invesment CEO since 2005, brings relentless drive for innovation and passion in personal wireless products. He founded RGI in 2005 and has stayed with the company ever since.

RGI Distribution is a subsidiary of Retail Group Investment, an authorized retailer for Cricket Wireless. We are a wholesale corporation in the U.S. based in Georgia that was established in 2007 as a small business that has grown tremendously over the years. We were among the early pioneers in cell phones, cell service and phone accessories. One store after another, and year upon year of sales experience have allowed us to craft our own brand of excellent customer service and professionalism. We have made durability, style, and top-notch customer service our top priority. We are proud to manufacture a wide array of cellular accessories that we design. We have been providing our customers with up-to-date and excellent quality cellular accessories for over a decade.

We stick strong to our motto “by the dealer, for the dealer.” Let us provide your customers with all of their mobile needs, from cutting-edge cellphone cases and speakers to Cricket gear. We strive to provide products that keep up with changing trends and creating unique styles for everyone. We are passionate about everything we do from our product innovation to the relationships we have with our customers. Over the years, we have created a brand that our customers know will deliver a reliable product.
